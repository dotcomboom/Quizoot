How to export from Quizlet

For best results, keep your terms less than 95 characters and definitions less than 65 characters. Note that these numbers include prefixes and suffixes if you set them later.

If you have trouble, change "Between term and definition" to something less commonly used, like a pipe (|). Don't forget to export again on Quizlet with the same settings.
